Welcome To The Guessing Game




RULES:

  • The Computer has a random number between 1 and 20.
  • As a beginner, You have 4 chances to guess the number.
  • Each time you guess a number you will know whether the number chosen by you is greater, smaller or equal.
  • If you guess the correct number you will WIN the game.
